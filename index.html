INTRODUCTION

BLACKPINK is a globally reowned South Korean girl group formed by YG Entertainment, consisting of four members: Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa. They are known for their powerful performances, catchy music, and strong visual identity. They debuted on August 8, 2016, with the digital single album SQUARE ONE, featuring the tracks "Boombayah" and "Whistle." The release quickly established them as one of the most prominent acts in the K-pop industry. They have a massive global fanbase called BLINK.
